How to Use

  1. Open any chapter in the reader
  2. Look for "Text Size:" controls in the header
  3. Click your preferred size:
    • A- for smaller text (16px)
    • A for default text (18px)
    • A+ for larger text (20px)
    • A++ for extra large text (22px)
  4. The change applies instantly and is saved automatically
